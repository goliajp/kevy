## §4 Performance (hot-path cement)

kevy-store IS on the per-command hot path. perf data lives in:
- `perfs/data/2026-05-26/metal-baseline.txt` (v0.metal-1 baseline)
- `perfs/data/2026-05-26/metal-3-value-inlining-ab.txt`
- `perfs/data/2026-05-26/metal-4-5-kevy-map-and-prefetch-ab.txt`
- `perfs/data/2026-05-26/metal-13-key-inline-ab.txt`

Per CEMENT-AUDIT §4, a per-cement BUDGETS.md is preferred — kevy-store
defers to the system-end metal_keyspace.sh A/B in `perfs/` for the
authoritative hot-path numbers, since the cement's per-op behavior is
dominated by the integrated keyspace path (Store::get → KevyMap →
SmallBytes), measured end-to-end, not in isolation. perf_gate.rs has
4 tests for per-op ns budgets (debug-gated to release).

A BUDGETS.md aggregating these would be a useful publish-prep artifact
but is not required for cement bar; flagged for future.
