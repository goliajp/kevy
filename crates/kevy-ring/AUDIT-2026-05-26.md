## Notes

- **Cross-thread perf is arch-sensitive**: 6-10 ns/item on lx64 x86_64
  vs ~63 ns/item on mac M-series. BUDGETS records the lx64 numbers as
  the production targets; mac numbers are dev-box readings only.
- **loom-vs-miri tradeoff**: ideally both. Miri walks a single schedule
  per run (no enumeration); loom enumerates all possible schedules
  exhaustively. For a SPSC ring with 4 atomics and standard orderings,
  the schedule space loom would explore is small enough that "manually
  reason + miri check" is acceptable. Document so the next auditor
  doesn't have to re-derive.
