## Notes

- **miri fix found + applied during audit**: `prefetch_t0`'s inline asm /
  arch intrinsics now `cfg(not(miri))`-gated so miri can model the
  semantic contract (prefetch is a hint = may no-op). This is a real
  pre-existing flaw in unsafe-correctness testability that the audit
  surfaced and fixed.
- **Coverage gap (87.84% vs 90%)**: investigate which lines aren't hit.
  Likely targets: error paths in `grow` (which can't easily be triggered
  in tests), Default impls, peripheral methods. Could be cheap to close
  with a couple of targeted tests.
- **Biggest BAR gap is the missing benches**: a Swiss-table stone
  without a vs-`std::HashMap` / vs-`hashbrown` micro-bench has no
  external credibility number. This should be the first BAR fix.
