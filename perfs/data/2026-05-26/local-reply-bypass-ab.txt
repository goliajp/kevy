io_uring sharded suite 11/11 with the bypass; full workspace tests + clippy green.

The bypass: when a single-key command runs on the conn's own shard AND nothing
is pending (its reply is the next to emit), dispatch_into() writes the RESP reply
straight into conn.output — skipping PendingSlot push/pop + fold + materialize +
the per-command reply Vec alloc + the drain copy. Attacks the post-Fx profile's
fold (9.5%) + reply malloc. Mixed cross-core (pending non-empty) keeps the
ordered slot path, so correctness (reply ordering) is preserved (sharded
pipeline-order + cross-shard tests pass).
